Medgen

June 2024 - August 2024

Medgen is a comprehensive Medicare-related platform designed to assist users in finding affordable alternatives to brand-name medications and locating nearby stores that carry these alternatives. The application was developed using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) and features a user-friendly interface that enables users to search for generic drug options and view detailed information about their efficacy and pricing. A key feature of Medgen is its robust search functionality, powered by Elasticsearch, which allows for quick and accurate retrieval of medication data. The platform also integrates a map API to help users easily locate stores within their vicinity that stock the required medications. On the backend, Node.js and Express.js manage the server-side logic and API interactions, while MongoDB serves as the primary database for storing medication and store information. Additionally, Cloudinary is used for managing and optimizing images, ensuring that product visuals are delivered quickly and efficiently. Medgen serves as a vital tool for users looking to save on prescription costs while ensuring they have access to necessary medications, all within an intuitive and reliable platform.

Smart Flow, The Traffic Guardian System

April 2024 - May 2024

Smart Flow is an innovative traffic management system designed for the GLB Hackathon 3.0, where our team secured a position among the top 20 teams in India. This project aimed to revolutionize urban traffic control by integrating AI into traditional traffic lights, enabling real-time monitoring and dynamic control of traffic flow and volume. Utilizing the YOLOv5 model for object detection, the system accurately identifies vehicles, assesses traffic density, and adjusts signal timings to optimize traffic flow, reducing congestion and improving overall road safety. As the team leader, I was responsible for designing and developing the entire backend infrastructure, ensuring seamless communication between the AI model and the traffic light system. Additionally, I managed the AI integration, ensuring that the system could process real-time video feeds and make data-driven decisions. The project also involved frontend state management using React, providing an intuitive interface for monitoring traffic conditions and system performance. Smart Flow exemplifies the potential of AI in addressing real-world challenges and demonstrates the effectiveness of technology in improving urban infrastructure.
